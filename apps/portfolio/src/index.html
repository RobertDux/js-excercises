About Me

Hello! My name is Robert and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My career in web development started back in 2014 when I decided to start my own web design and development company, called Webbify.

Now, ten years later, I am a consultant at Dux Group where I advise organisations on all aspects of data. I've also been focussing on building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences at Dux Group for internal use.
